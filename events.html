Events

Get to know the E-Cell and the events it conducts

The Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT(BHU) is institute run body run by the students of IIT(BHU) Varanasi devoted towards acting as a symbiotic link between entrepreneurs of E-Cell,IIT(BHU) and the existing startup ecosystem as well acting as a hub where all startups can meet, collaborate and innovate.

What is the Cell about?

The Entrepreneurship-Cell woks towards strengthening ties with the willing and talented entrepreneurs towards a development of startup hub in Varanasi providing numerous opportunities and top quality education in the future. The Entrepreneurship Cell as a whole will be responsible for the apposite interaction between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell,IIT(BHU) and the startup ecosystem.

On its mission towards building an entreprising India, Entrepreneurship Cell IIT BHU provides great opportunities for startups, colleges, alumni and corporates to get involved with us. The Structure of our E-Cell represents our concern over different aspects of our vision.

Know the Events

E-Summit


Entrepreneurship Summit is an excellent opportunity for young and aspiring entrepreneurs to turn their startup dreams into reality. This extravagant event provides a perfect platform for the unsung heroes of our EC to interact with prospective investors by pitcher 2-3/5 ideas. E-Summit aspires to help the enthusiasts gain knowledge from people who have seen the in and out of the entrepreneurial sector over many years. So, what are you waiting for? Join us to think, ideate, express, achieve, and get ready to step into this Festival to sneak at how the future looks like.



Startup Weekend


Startup Weekend, Varanasi, powered by Google, is organized every year usually by an independent team in association with The Science and Technology Council and The Business Club, for enthusiastic Entrepreneurs. Due to the event’s orientation towards entrepreneurship, it was included in the Constitution of E-Cell, and hence E-Cell also became a part of the organizing team at the time of its establishment. This event aims to learn together and network with each other, thus, helping with the ideation stage of the startups.



HULT Prize


The HULT Prize is organized in association with the ‘Club of Sustainability and Innovation’ and ‘The Business Club’. This event is oriented towards social entrepreneurship and is a big hit with two eliminatory rounds - Abstract Submission and Prelims, following which one team - Speedofy is selected for the Global Finals from the top 10 teams that pitch in the event. Globally, it is run by the Bill Clinton Foundation and the HULT International Business School, Cambridge, Massachusetts.For more than a decade, the Hult Prize Foundation has been transforming how young people envision their own possibilities as impact leaders of change in the world around them.
