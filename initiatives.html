Initiatives

Get to know the E-Cell and the Initiatives taken

Initiatives by the E-Cell Team

Internship Portal Internship Portal

The internship portal is an initiative taken by the E-Cell IIT BHU, aiming to bridge the gap between students and startups. The Internship portal will connect startups across the country with the students of IIT(BHU), Varanasi. Each startup listed on this portal will offer a role in their company, for students with the same vision and passion. This platform will offer students the opportunity to interact with the ever-growing startup ecosystem and for the startups to benefit from the expertise and skills of the students.



Mentorship Program Mentorship Program

This Program aims to help Freshers to learn the basics of Entrepreneurship in small groups of around 20 freshers and 2 Mentors. Mentors are the Sophomores associated with E-Cell working with different teams to maintain the working of E-Cell. The Groups help Freshers in inculcating Book Reading Habit and Group Discussion regularly. Every weekend around 320 Freshers and 30+ Mentors meet for an hour or two to share their learnings, opinions & experiences, in their respective groups. This program creates a community to help the freshers network with each other and communicate their ideas.



Startup 101 Program Startup 101 Program

Startup 101 is a lecture series that aims at making everyone aware of the basics of Entrepreneurship & Startup ecosystem of the country. Cutting all the clutter, jargons & complexities, this series aims to give beginners substantial takeaways through the lectures. This program wants the participants to start expressing their ideas and their world view towards different problems of the world. The talks are conducted on various topics ranging from Research to Prototyping and Product Launch.



Founders Speak Founders Speak

The Founder’s speak is a weekly program curated by E-Cell, IIT(BHU), Varanasi, where successful and serial entrepreneurs share their journey with the startup ecosystem. It is a platform where Entrepreneurs share precious insights from the Business World. The primary goal of this event is to build awareness around the startup culture and provide students with a better overall view of the Startup Ecosystem.



Know Your Department Know Your Department

Know Your Department (KYD) is a series of sessions of each department that enables the students to have an entrepreneurial perspective of the respective branch's core field. The main objective of the event is to address 3 important topics for the 1st and 2nd-year students that are core job prospects, Research opportunities, and Entrepreneurial scopes. 'Know Your Department' will help students to get a much broader initial outlook on the different opportunities in their field and work on their interests early on.



Campus Director Program Campus Director Program

Let’s be the voice of change through the Campus Director Program of E-Cell IIT (BHU), Varanasi, in your college. The primary vision for launching the CA Programme is the development of an Entrepreneurial Culture among the students of various colleges across India. We aim at developing future leaders with an entrepreneurial mindset, well-versed in various skills including leadership, public speaking, and business development. With Free seats in various events and lecture series given by various founders and CEOs, CA Program aims at all-round development as an individual.
