About Us

Get to know the E-Cell and the purposes it serves

The Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT(BHU), Varanasi, is an institute run body, run by the students of IIT(BHU), Varanasi, devoted towards acting as a symbiotic link between entrepreneurs of E-Cell, IIT(BHU), Varanasi, and the existing startup ecosystem as well acting as a hub where all startups can meet, collaborate and innovate.

What is the E-Cell about?

The Entrepreneurship Cell works towards strengthening ties with the willing and talented entrepreneurs towards the development of startup hub in Varanasi providing numerous opportunities and top-quality education in the future. The Entrepreneurship Cell as a whole will be responsible for the apposite interaction between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell, IIT(BHU), Varanasi, and the startup ecosystem.

On its mission towards building an enterprising India, Entrepreneurship Cell IIT BHU provides great opportunities for startups, colleges, alumni and corporates to get involved with us. The Structure of our E-Cell represents our concern over different aspects of our vision.

Strategic Relations team Strategic Relations team


This team is responsible for the establishment of relationships with external organizations for the development and growth of the startups. Start-up Assistance Program Team Start-up Assistance Program Team


This unit assists the startups in brainstorming during the ideation stage and discusses the feasibility and scope of improvement of startup ideas with entrepreneurs. This team plays a key role in bridging the gap between the entrepreneurs and the skilled personnel they requires such as developers, designers, content writers, etc. This team guides the startups by providing mentorship at the time of product development, making them aware of the competition, and informing them of the mistakes their competitors have made in the past. Along with the guidance, the team ensures the proper marketing and publicity of the products and services offered by startups.

Cisco thingQbator Cisco thingQbator


Cisco thingQbator is one of the pillars of E-Cell, IIT(BHU) that strives towards creating a network of makerspaces where students can learn about digital technologies in a hands-on environment and turn their ideas into working prototypes. The primary aim of Thinqbator is to create makerspaces where IoT enthusiasts could learn more about digital technologies in a hands-on environment and turn their ideas into working prototypes. Through its CSR partner - NASSCOM Foundation, Cisco partnered with 18 universities across India, including IIT(BHU) Varanasi, as a part of its Corporate Social Responsibility commitments in India from 2018 - 2021. Over the course of the Cohort, the 75 teams will get access to the Virtual Makerspace and provide a host of tools, mentorship, and business and technology training. The Cohort culminates with a demo day wherein the top 30 teams showcase their products in action, and the top 10 winners get seed funding of Rs. 5 Lakhs each along with incubation at a GOI-approved TBI.
